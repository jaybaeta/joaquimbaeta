--- title: Weave ---

Weave and weave.

Escape,

Through the waterfall.

Light will guide you.

Leap into the air,

Believe,

And run.

Weave.

Through the weeds.

And run.

You are lost,

But light will guide you,

Through the blossoms,

Spiral toward the mountain.

Through the waterfall,

Weave.

And find your way,

If you believe.

Wait.

Wait.

Now!

Run.

Leap.

Dive.

Run.

Find the light,

And let it guide you.

Down the mountain,

Through the waterfall,

Through the weeds.

Weave,

And you will be lost.
